Cardio Equipment

Our range of cardio equipment, including treadmills, cross trainers, stair climbers, steppers, exercise bikes and rowing machines, will improve your endurance and stamina

Weights Area

From dumbbells up to 50kg (in most gyms), weights benches and squat racks, to plate-loaded equipment your fitness will go from strength to strength.

Fixed Resistance Weights

Fixed-weight machines are a great place to start weight training before you’ve developed the skills required for training with free weights.
